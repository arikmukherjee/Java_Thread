About Us

The Art of Reasoned Discourse

Founded in 1997, the Veritas Debate Society has been the intellectual crucible of the University of Excellence. We train students to think critically, argue logically, and speak with conviction on the world's most pressing issues.

🎯
Mission
Cultivating fearless thinkers who can defend and challenge ideas with intellectual rigor and eloquence.
🔭
Vision
To be the nation's foremost university debate society, producing leaders who shape democratic discourse.
⚖️
Values
Truth, intellectual humility, rigorous research, and respect for opposing perspectives.
1997
Society founded with 12 charter members by Professor A. Singh.
2003
First National Championship title at the All-India Debate League.
2012
Launched annual intercollege tournament — now with 60+ institutions.
2024
Reached 450+ active members; won three consecutive national titles.
